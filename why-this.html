Why this?

It is still special for me to take advantage of this website to talk about books.

I have been working as a bookseller for some years, so talking about books is something common in my day-to-day life. It is a profession that teaches you the value of knowledge and the importance of its dissemination. Even more so in a country like mine (Colombia) where reading rates are low, and the cost of books is high.

Recently I have opted for multimedia narratives as an attractive possibility to promote reading. I understand reading as an opportunity to learn about everything, not just literature, history or humanities in general. Science too! And here I am learning programming to build interactive sites, digital museums or video games where you can experience the joy of learning. That's what this is about!
